About Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology

Rajalakshmi Institute of Technology (RIT), located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, is an esteemed autonomous engineering institution established in 2008. It is affiliated with Anna University and approved by AICTE. RIT is recognized for its commitment to providing quality education in various engineering fields, and its state-of-the-art infrastructure and academic excellence make it a prime choice for aspiring engineers.

Courses Offered

RIT offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs to cater to the dynamic needs of the industry:

  • B.Tech. Artificial Intelligence & Data Science
  • B.Tech. Computer Science and Business Systems
  • B.E. Computer & Communication Engineering
  • B.E. Computer Science & Engineering
  • B.E. Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • B.E. Mechanical Engineering
  • B.E. Computer Science & Engineering (AI & ML)
  • B.E. Electronics Engineering (VLSI Design & Technology)
  • M.E. VLSI Design
  • M.B.A. Logistics and Supply Chain Management

University Rankings

RIT has been consistently ranked by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), placing it among the top engineering institutions in India. In the 2024 rankings, RIT was ranked between 151-200 in the engineering category.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

RIT offers modern amenities and infrastructure, including:

  • Advanced labs and research centers
  • Sports facilities (volleyball, badminton, cricket, track field)
  • Library with e-books and journals
  • Wi-Fi enabled campus
  • Collaborations with industries and foreign universities

Research and Development

RIT actively promotes research and innovation. The institution has multiple collaborations with industry leaders and academic bodies to foster research in emerging technologies. Students and faculty regularly engage in cutting-edge research, with several publications and projects in AI, Machine Learning, and VLSI design.

Placements and Internships

RIT has a strong placement record, with top companies recruiting from the campus. In 2023, the highest salary offered was INR 10 LPA, with a median of INR 5.16 LPA. Leading companies like Virtusa, Zoho, and TCS visit the campus regularly for placements.
